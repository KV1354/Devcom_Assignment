Sounding the Alarm...

What wretched soul dares to shatter the tranquility of my realm?

Ah, 'tis none other than thee, Devcom, the harbinger of disturbance!

Dost thou possess not an iota of respect for my solitude?

Nevertheless, since thou hast ventured forth with such brazen impudence, I suppose I

shall permit thee a glimpse into the grandeur of my humble abode.

But mark my words, tread lightly, for thou art but a mere mortal in the presence of

greatness!
